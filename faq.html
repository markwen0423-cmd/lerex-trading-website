FAQ

Common questions from international customers

Below are questions about how we work, what makes us different, and how to start a sourcing project.

Do you only sell products?

No. Our approach is service-first. We help customers with product understanding, supplier coordination, quality and timing follow-up, not just selling.

Why does physical store experience matter?

Because real store and workshop experience helps us understand better how products are used, installed, judged by customers, and what drives repurchase demand.

Can you help with stable quality and timing?

Yes. We care strongly about stability and try to set realistic quality expectations and reliable timing communication.

Do you support sampling before bulk orders?

Yes. Sample work and requirement confirmation are normal parts of practical sourcing support.

Can you help with export and shipment?

Yes. We can support packaging, documentation, scheduling, and communication with related parties.

How do we start working together?

Send us your product category, specification, target market, volume, and project priorities. We'll review everything and reply with the next practical step.

Still have questions?

If your sourcing process, target category, or export arrangement needs further discussion, send us your project details and we'll reply with practical suggestions.
